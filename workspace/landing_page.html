Geothermal gradient, thermal conductivity

To determine the marine heat flow distribution in the Chukchi Basin (Chukchi Abyssal Plain), we obtained in situ geothermal gradients at five stations and laboratory-measured thermal conductivities at three stations along an east–west transect across the Chukchi Abyssal Plain.
